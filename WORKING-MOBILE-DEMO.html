Fr. Joseph Richetti Catholic School

Excellence in Education Since 2005

About Our Academy

Fr. Joseph Richetti Catholic School was founded in 2005 by Italian Consolata Priest Fr. Joseph Richetti. Our mission is to provide quality Catholic education while nurturing the spiritual, intellectual, and moral development of each student.

We currently serve 776 students across Pre-School (135) and Primary School (472), maintaining small class sizes with a 15:1 student-teacher ratio.

Admissions

We welcome applications throughout the year. Our admissions process is designed to be simple and straightforward, ensuring that every family has the opportunity to join our community.

Academic Programs

Our curriculum follows the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) framework, offering comprehensive education from Pre-School through Primary School.

Achievements

Our students consistently achieve 100% placement in secondary schools, demonstrating our commitment to academic excellence.
