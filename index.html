School Fees & Payment

Payment via M-Pesa Paybill: 522533 (Account: Student Name). 70% payment required before term begins. Additional fees may apply for extracurricular activities and special programs.

Academic Calendar & Terms

Our academic year runs from January to November, divided into three terms. First term: January-April, Second term: May-August, Third term: September-November. Each term includes comprehensive assessments and parent-teacher conferences.

School operates Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM. We follow the Kenyan national curriculum with additional Catholic values integration. Holiday breaks are scheduled between terms for family time and student rest.
